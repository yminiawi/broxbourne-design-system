Brand foundations

Purpose

To make Broxbourne the best place to live, work and visit

This high-level statement is not specific to any one service. It encompasses the Council as a whole as well as providing a focal point for its vision, values and daily operations

In more detail:

“To make Broxbourne the best place”

Broxbourne is an innovative and forward thinking council striving to be the best Borough for all its stakeholders.

“to live, work and visit”

People are a key focus for the Council; those living in, working in and with and visiting the Borough. This encompasses residents, business owners, tourists and staff.

This purpose statement is the basis of the Council’s vision. The vision has freedom to evolve and develop while still being grounded in this purpose.

Vision

“We are Broxbourne” Making a difference by delivering the best service to everyone, every day

The vision is a reality; the Council leads by example. It is measurable, and designed to evolve and develop. It should provide an aspirational goal for everyone working at the Council.

In more detail:

“We are Broxbourne”

They key headline: it unifies all people involved with the Council.

“Making a difference by delivering the best service”

This aligns to the purpose of the Council — to deliver high quality services. This could be measured in either a qualitative or quantitative way.

“to everyone, every day”

In this way the vision resonates with those working for the Council and those who engage with it.

Values

Beliefs and principles which guide behaviour. These values stem from the 2030 leader’s vision and from the housing team, mission statement and values:

Improving the lives of our customers

  • having a positive attitude
  • being respectful
  • listening
  • willing to innovate

Delivering good quality services

  • being supportive
  • using initiative
  • accepting difference
  • being adaptable
  • being solution-focused

Providing value for money

  • attention to detail
  • quality with affordability
  • right first time

High performing

  • achieving goals
  • taking responsibility
  • working as a team

Tone of Voice

The writing style for Broxbourne's Digital Services should reflect the following characteristics:

  • a cool, not overly passionate tone
  • a forward-thinking perspective
  • a compassionate stance
  • a formal and respectful demeanour
  • a strong belief in our values and vision

The personal pronoun will be ‘the Council’ (singular) with a capital C. The Council's customers should be referred to in the second person (you). Active voice at all times, and use ‘Please’ wherever the user is being asked to complete a task.
